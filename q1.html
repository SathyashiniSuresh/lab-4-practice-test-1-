Explanation

the use of strong and emphasis tags is a better choice than bold and italic tags because they provide semantic meaning to the content. While strong highlights importance content, and emphasizes it, while bold and italic only change visual appearance. Screen readers recognize strong and emphasis and convey their importance, improving the experience for visually impaired users.This makes them more meaningful than the purely stylistic bold and italic tags.
